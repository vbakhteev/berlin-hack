Track Inca – The Human Test

An AI voice agent that handles insurance claims calls end-to-end.
Your customers think it's a human. Your accountant knows it's not.

The Problem

Insurance companies receive thousands of First Notice of Loss (FNOL) calls daily. Each call requires a trained agent to gather claim details, verify policy coverage, and create a ticket — a repetitive, expensive process that doesn't scale.

Our Solution

Lina — an AI voice agent that answers insurance calls and completes full FNOL intake automatically:

  • Verifies policy coverage in real-time
  • Collects incident details, date, location
  • Files the claim via structured tool calls
  • Speaks German & English natively

The key insight: strategic imperfection. Lina is modeled as a 62-year-old agent with 27 years of experience — flat delivery, deliberate pauses, zero enthusiasm. Callers trust her because she doesn't sound like AI.

Tech Stack

  • Gemini Live API (real-time voice streaming)
  • Next.js + TypeScript
  • Convex (real-time claim database)
  • Custom phone audio DSP (EQ, compression, noise floor simulation)

Built For

AXA Zurich Allianz

Any insurer handling inbound FNOL at scale.

How to Test It

  1. Open berlin-hack-psi.vercel.app/axa on your phone or laptop
  2. On the home screen, tap the blue phone button — "Schaden melden / Report a claim"
  3. Lina answers. Speak naturally — tell her you had an incident (e.g. "My laptop was stolen" or "Mein Laptop wurde gestohlen")
  4. She'll ask follow-up questions to complete the claim: date, location, description
  5. Once done, the claim summary appears automatically on screen

Works in German and English. No login required. Allow microphone access when prompted.
